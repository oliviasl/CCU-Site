Our Mission

We are a club at Mountain View High School that strives to support local clinics in their pursuit to provide free healthcare services for the uninsured and underserved in our community.



We will do this by fundraising for the RotaCare Bay Area organization, a collection of local clinics that provide free healthcare services to homeless and low income individuals.



What is RotaCare Bay Area organization?

The RotaCare Bay Area organization is a collection of local clinics that provide free healthcare services to homeless and low income individuals.



Their funds go towards:

•   Healthcare

•   Lab Testing

•   Diagnostic Testing

•   Medical Examinations

•   Medication

Their locations include:

•   Coastside

•   Concord

•   Gilroy

•   Monterey

•   Northern Peninsula

•   Pittsburg

•   Richmond

•   San Jose

•   San Rafael

•   Santa Cruz

More information at www.rotacarebayarea.org
